Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Invoice Maker ("the App") is developed and maintained by an independent developer. This policy describes how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers. All data you create, including invoices, estimates, client information, and business details, is stored locally on your device.

Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as screen views, feature usage frequency, and app performance metrics. This data contains no personally identifiable information and is used only to improve the app experience. No advertising identifier is collected.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics - anonymous usage analytics (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Adapty - subscription and in-app purchase management (Adapty Privacy Policy)

These services may collect anonymous technical data such as device type, OS version, and app version as described in their own privacy policies.

Subscriptions

The App may offer optional premium features via auto-renewable subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. The developer does not have access to your payment information.

Data Storage and Security

Invoices, estimates, clients, and business information are stored locally on your device. The App does not provide cloud storage or sync. If you delete the App, locally stored data will be permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact mintinvoiceapp@gmail.com.
