Subscriptions

Invoice Maker offers optional in-app subscriptions for Pro features. Billing is handled by Apple through your App Store account.

  • To restore access, open the app and go to the More tab, then choose Restore Purchases.
  • To manage or cancel your subscription, open your Apple ID subscription settings from the App Store.
  • If a purchase is pending, access unlocks automatically after Apple confirms the transaction.

Documents and Data

Your invoices, estimates, clients, and business details are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes this local data from the device.
