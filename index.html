Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Invoice Maker & Estimate - PDF ("the App") is developed and maintained by an independent developer. This policy describes how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you create — invoices, estimates, client information, and business details — is stored locally on your device only.

Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as screen views, feature usage frequency, and app performance metrics. This data contains no personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve the App experience. No advertising identifier (IDFA) is collected.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics (see Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Adapty — subscription and in-app purchase management (see Adapty Privacy Policy)

These services may collect anonymous technical data (device type, OS version, app version) as described in their respective privacy policies.

Subscriptions

The App may offer optional premium features via auto-renewable subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. The developer does not have access to your payment information.

Data Storage & Security

All your invoices, estimates, clients, and business information are stored locally on your device using the app's file system. The App does not use cloud storage or sync services. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at mintinvoiceapp@gmail.com.
